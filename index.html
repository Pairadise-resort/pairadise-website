Where you'll be

Quiet, but close to everything

Pairadise sits in Baan Mae Yen, just outside the heart of Pai. You're tucked into gardens, mountain views, and natural water, yet only a 10-15 minute walk or a short scooter ride to Pai walking street, cafes, and night market.

It's the best of both: peaceful sleep at night, and the buzz of town whenever you want it.
